Tomato Pasta

4 servings 30 min Nonna's recipe book
Edit
Vegetarian Spicy

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 400 g Rigatoni

Sauce

  • 500 g Canned Tomatoes
  • 3 ea Garlic Cloves
  • Salt untracked

Directions

1

Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil.

2

Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.

For the sauce

1

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.

2

Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Add tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.

3

Drain pasta and toss with sauce. Season to taste.
