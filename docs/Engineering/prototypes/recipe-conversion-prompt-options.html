Recipe ingredient unit conversion — surfacing the cross-dimension gap (C10 / R7)

When a tracked ingredient's unit can't be converted to the product's stock unit, Plantry needs a one-time density factor (so cook / fulfillment / costing math works). Today this is discovered only after you hit "Create recipe". These options detect it at unit-select time, inside the add sheet, and reframe the ask. Type in the boxes; click the escape link.

Scenario: new recipe, adding Flour (catalogued in g). You enter 1 and pick unit cup → cross-dimension gap. (Same-dimension picks like kg resolve automatically and never prompt.)
